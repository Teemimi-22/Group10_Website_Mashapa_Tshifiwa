About Us:


Empowering the Nation is an initiative by Precious Radebe to provide skills training for domestic workers and gardeners. It was established in 2018 and offers courses in Johannesburg. Our small- to mid-size enterprise has trained over hundreds of domestic workers and gardeners on both the six-months long Learnership and six-week Short Training Programmers to empower themselves and can provide more marketable skills.
